AI Text App

Welcome to the AI Text App! This app leverages the power of Artificial Intelligence APIs to provide concise summaries of long texts or the ability to generate images from text! Whether you have a lengthy article or any other text document that you want to summarize quickly, or generate AI images of a text description or of a book scene, our app can assist you.

Simply paste your text into the text area below and click the button below to summarize or generate image.
